The Joke That Explains Itself

Why $8? Van Buren was the 8th President Why Van Buren? He was born in Kinderhook Why "OK"? His nickname was "Old Kinderhook" — origin of "OK" Can you spend it? Yes. At any participating business. For $8.

Why This Works

Memes spread. Currency is inherently shareable. The $8 bill is absurd enough to screenshot but real enough to visit. Every share is free advertising for Kinderhook. Every tourist who comes for the meme stays for the village.
