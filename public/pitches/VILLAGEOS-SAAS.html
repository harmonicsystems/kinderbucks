The Problem

Tourism dollars leak

Visitors come, walk around, leave. No capture. No data. No return visits. Money flows to Airbnb and Amazon, not Main Street.

No digital infrastructure

Big cities have apps, maps, loyalty programs. Small towns have... a Facebook page from 2016 and a paper brochure at the gas station.

Tech is intimidating

Local business owners aren't developers. They can't build apps. They shouldn't have to.

No collective intelligence

Each business is an island. No one knows where visitors go, what they want, or how to bring them back.

The Solution

VillageOS is a managed operating system for small-town tourism.

We deploy a complete digital layer—loyalty cards, QR-code check-ins, local currency, visitor tracking, gamified exploration—and we run it for you. Your job: hang posters and give discounts. Our job: everything else.

Think of it as Shopify for villages. You don't build the infrastructure. You just use it.

Data Layers We Deploy

Loyalty Layer

Resident discount cards. Visitor passes. Tiered rewards. Streaks and badges.

Location Layer

QR codes at landmarks, trails, businesses. Check-ins that build a map of movement.

Currency Layer

Local currency (physical or digital). Keeps dollars circulating. 2-3x multiplier effect.

Game Layer

Scavenger hunts. Seasonal quests. Collectible badges. Completionist bundles.

Content Layer

History tours. Nature guides. Story unlocks. Every QR is a portal to context.

Analytics Layer

Foot traffic patterns. Popular routes. Return visit rates. Shared openly with the village.
