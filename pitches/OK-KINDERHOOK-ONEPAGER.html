OK Kinderhook

A Local Economic Stimulus Program for the Village
Free to Build & Operate

OK Membership

  • Free for all village residents
  • Exclusive discounts at participating businesses
  • Businesses set their own discount level
  • Digital card + optional physical backup

Check-In System

  • Scan QR code at any business
  • Earn streaks, badges, achievements
  • Seasonal discovery trails & challenges
  • Exchange badges for Kinderhooker swag

Kinderbucks Currency

  • Physical local currency (like BerkShares)
  • Accepted 1:1 with USD at businesses
  • Special "Kinderbucks-only" deals
  • Keeps dollars circulating locally

For Businesses

  • Zero cost to participate
  • Set your own discounts & promos
  • Analytics on visit patterns
  • Free listing in OK directory
