~$1,500

1. Member Discounts

Village residents get an OK Member card. Show it for discounts at local businesses.

Like a AAA card for Kinderhook.

2. Visit Tracking

Each visit gets recorded. See which shops you've supported this month.

Like a digital punch card.

3. Earn Rewards

Visit regularly, earn badges. Complete challenges, earn bonuses.

Like frequent flyer miles for Main Street.

4. Kinderbucks Currency

Physical local dollars accepted at participating businesses. Special deals for Kinderbucks holders.

Like BerkShares in the Berkshires.

  • Free membership
