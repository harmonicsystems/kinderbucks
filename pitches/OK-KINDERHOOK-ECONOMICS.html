Three Principles of Habit Economics

Frequency Beats Margin

A customer who comes weekly at 10% off is worth more than one who comes monthly at full price. Volume × frequency > margin × occasional.

Defaults Win

People don't decide where to shop—they default. Make local the habit and you've won before the "decision" happens.

Loyalists Recruit

Habitual customers tell friends. One loyal customer brings 2-3 more. Premium-seeking tourists don't do that.
