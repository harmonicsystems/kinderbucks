Columbia County & Beyond

Every Village, A Collection

The Disneyland layer for Hudson Valley day trips

What if every village had its own infinite art universe?

Catskill has painted cat statues. Charming, but expensive. Static. Hard to maintain. What if instead, every village had hundreds of collectible digital artworks-created by local artists, refreshed seasonally, discovered by scanning QR codes throughout town?

Physical Installations

  • Expensive to create
  • Weather damage
  • Theft/vandalism
  • Fixed quantity
  • Can't update
  • One-time experience
VS

Digital Collections

  • Cheap to produce
  • Weatherproof (it's pixels)
  • Can't be stolen
  • Infinite variations
  • Seasonal updates
  • Reason to return

Local Artists Run the Universe

Every piece of art is created by a Hudson Valley artist. They get credited, paid, and discovered. The collection stays fresh because new artists are always contributing.

Artist Stipends

$50-200 per illustration, depending on complexity. Ongoing commissions for seasonal updates.

Attribution Everywhere

Every collectible links to the artist's portfolio. Visitors discover local talent while exploring.

Style Diversity

Watercolor cats. Pixel art foxes. Vintage whales. Every artist brings their own voice.

Always Fresh

New artists, new styles, new seasonal drops. There's always a reason to come back.
