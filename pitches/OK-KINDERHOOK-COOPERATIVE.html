OK Kinderhook

A Village Digital Cooperative
Use Your Money. Use Your Body. Use Your Mind.
A villager challenge: Like workplace wellness programs, but for our whole community. Shop local, walk the village, learn our history - and track your progress together.

Use Your Money

OK Member discounts at local businesses. Kinderbucks currency keeps dollars circulating here - not leaking to big box stores.

Use Your Body

Walking challenges. Rail trail adventures. Discovery trails through the village. Earn rewards by exploring on foot.

Use Your Mind

Seasonal storylines through village history. Learn about Mohican heritage, Dutch settlers, African American history, and more.

QR Codes Are Portals

Simple codes placed around the village connect people to whatever we want - and we can update them anytime:

Local businesses
Historic sites
Walking tours
Rail trail access
Seasonal events
Art installations
Nature spots
Story chapters

Community Intelligence

When people participate, we learn how the village moves: Which businesses thrive? Which corners need attention? Where do visitors go?

Data owned by the village, not corporations.

Radical Transparency

This is a digital cooperative. The village controls it. All data is shared openly with residents. No hidden algorithms. No selling information. Just insights to help our community grow.

For Everyone, Regardless of Age or Ability

Free demonstrations at the Kinderhook Memorial Library. Physical cards for those without smartphones. Large print materials. We meet people where they are.
